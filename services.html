Services We Offer

LAND: LandSolve assists clients in acquiring clear titled land parcels for buying, selling and joint venture at Bangalore and some other tier two cities of Karnataka. We respect the privacy of our clients by sharing the property details ONLY with the actual buyers. We do NOT share info with real estate agents in the market, in order to avoid misrepresentation of the property & circulation of the related papers.

LIASONING: We are one of the most favored and top most land liasoners in Bangalore. We assist in obtaining any kind of land papers such as Change of land use, Conversions, Zone changing, Khata , Pani , Podi, NOC’s, AC or DC Orders, Sale Permissions, Approvals, Sanction plans, Modification of existing plans, Permissions etc. Our capability is such that we can precisely get any sort of work done, at various levels of the departments of the government & local authorities if legal.

LITIGATION: We respect and maintain confidentiality of the client and look into settlements and litigation resolving with government authorities, family disputes, someone else in possession of your property etc, if you are the actual legal beneficiary of the property.
